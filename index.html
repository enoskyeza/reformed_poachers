#About Us

The Journey of Uganda’s Reformed Poachers!

We are a growing movement of individuals who once hunted wildlife to survive — but now protect it with purpose. By empowering former poachers through education, skills training, and community outreach, we are rewriting the future of conservation in Uganda.
